Program Structure

How Our Semesters Are Structured

Our Programs Are Offered Year Round!

At Seedling, we run programs year-round, following the traditional school schedule. Enrollment from does not automatically carry over from one semester to the next and registration is required for each of the 3 semesters

Fall Semester

  • August to January
  • Two shows per class in January
  • Registration for classes starts early May

    • Spring Semester

  • February to May
  • Two shows per class in May
  • Registration for classes starts mid November

    • Summer Camps

  • June to July
  • One day and two week long camp options
  • Registration for classes starts mid March

    • Fall and Spring Semester Information

    What we offer each semester:
    + Acting Classes short semester--Aug-Nov & Feb-Apr:
    Jrs (ages 7-10) & Teens (ages 11-18)
    + Musical Theatre Classes for age groups:
    Sprouts (6-8yrs) Jrs (8-10yrs) Center Stage (10-12yrs) Teens (11-18yrs)
    + Vocal Performance Classes for age group 10-18yrs

    Summer Camp Information

    Summer camps vary but generally include at least one Musical production, full plays, vocal performance, Movie Making, camps for ages 4-6yrs, set design and costume design, beginning acting, and much more.

    Musical Productions

    Each age-specific Musical Theatre class will form its own independent cast of the musical production for that semester. If there are 5 classes offered for Lion King, there will be 5 full casts performing their own Lion King shows at the end of the semester. Auditions for character roles will take place in class the 2nd or 3rd week of each semester (with the exception of Showcase Cast) and all students enrolled in the class will be cast in the show.
